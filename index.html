    Serving: 4

  • 1/2 tuber yam (medium)

  • 1/2 cup dry oporo
  • 2 knorr cube
  • 1 tbsp crayfish grounded
  • 8 hobonero pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cksp palm oil
  • 2 cups diced scent leaf

  • Peel yam, cube it, wash and set aside

  • Wash and shred Pepper roughly and set aside

  • Wash oporo and soak then set aside
